Cancer Analysis

From 2009 to 2018, the rapid development of cancer research extended the depth or even expanded the dimension of research in that filed. The nature or complexity of the filed, however, impedes the laymen or students in life science to have a thorough understanding about cancer research. The analysis of the Pubmed articles, we did, could clearly present the changes of topics of interest or hot-spots in cancer research which can assist novice to interpret the area in a different but much efficient way.
